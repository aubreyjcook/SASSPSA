Summer Heat PSA
Home Hazards Emergency Contact

While most of the conditions associated with summer heat can be prevented by following some basic guidelines, turbulent weather conditions and uncontrollable circumstances can lead some people to critical condition. No matter what the circumstances, if it becomes clear that someone is suffering from a heat-related illness and you are unable to treat them promptly, medical professionals should be contacted for emergency assistance. Fortunately, most heat-related illnesses can be treated by professionals quickly and effectively. If in doubt, contact the number below immediately for help, don't wait until a friend or loved one reaches a critical condition that you will be unable to help them.
